Getting Here

Train: A fast train from London Waterloo takes around 45 mins. Check the National Rail website for times and prices. Basingstoke is also easy to get to by train from Winchester (15 mins), Oxford (50 mins), Salisbury (40 mins) and Southampton (30 mins). From Bath change trains at Reading.

Car/Uber/Taxi: If you are staying nearby, we can usually meet you at your accommodation and drop you back at the end of the day. If you are driving yourself, we can usually arrange for you to park close to our office. If you are coming direct from London Heathrow it is best to book a cab and it will take around 1 hour depending on traffic.

How to pay

We accept cash and most major credit and debit cards. Any balance will be payable on the day of your tour. We also accept BACS payments from invoiced clients.
